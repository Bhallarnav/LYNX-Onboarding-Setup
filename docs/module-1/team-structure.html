1.2 Team Structure & Who to Ask

You will have questions you don't know the answer to. That’s expected and normal. What matters is knowing who to ask — and asking before your client notices you don’t know.

note

Quick rule: If it affects a client’s timeline, money, or confidence — ask immediately.

note

WHAT TO SAY TO A CLIENT WHEN YOU DON’T HAVE THE ANSWER

“That’s a great question — I want to make sure I give you accurate information on that, so let me confirm with my team lead and get back to you within the hour.”

This is not weakness. This is professionalism. Clients respect agents who are honest about what they’re confirming — they lose trust in agents who guess wrong.

  • Never fabricate an answer.
  • Never delay telling them you’re checking.
  • Always follow up within the time you promised.
