title: 1.3 Your First 30 Days
---

:::note
This is your roadmap for month one. Work through it week by week and bring it to every check-in. Completion of this checklist is required before your 30-day review with your Mentor.

:::

---

## Week 1 - Get set up & get oriented

:::note
Goal: Fully operational on all tools, know the team, understand expectations.

:::

- [ ]  Download FUB app — notifications on, profile complete → see Module 2.1
- [ ]  Add 50+ contacts to your FUB database (everyone you know)
- [ ]  Confirm CREB MLS access — run a test search and save it → see Module 2.2
- [ ]  Join team Discord— introduce yourself in #general [https://discord.gg/ZJYvyPJJsh](https://discord.gg/ZJYvyPJJsh)
- [ ]  Create a Canva account if you don’t already have one
- [ ]  Download Cap-Cut
- [ ]  Complete handbook Modules 1 & 2 in full (watch all videos)
- [ ]  Shadow one live lead call with your mentor or a senior agent

---

## Week 2 - Learn the buyer side

:::note
Goal: Confident handling a buyer from first call to booked consultation.

:::

- [ ]  Complete Module 3 — Working with buyers (print the call script)
- [ ]  Buyer call roleplay with your mentor — run through the full script
- [ ]  Attend at least one showing with your mentor or any senior agent
- [ ]  Read a blank AREA purchase contract end to end — highlight anything unclear
- [ ]  Take your first real inbound lead call — mentor available as backup
- [ ]  Post your first social media content using a Canva template

---

## Week 3 - Learn the seller side

:::note
Goal: Understand the listing process and be able to build a basic CMA.

:::

- [ ]  Complete Module 4 — Working with sellers (pay attention to the pricing conversation)
- [ ]  Attend a listing appointment as an observer — take notes, debrief after
- [ ]  Build your first CMA in MLS — bring it to your week 3 check-in for review
- [ ]  Reach out to 10 people in your sphere (reconnect, not a sales pitch)
- [ ]  Week 3 check-in — bring your CMA and listing appointment notes

---

## Week 4 — Start flying solo

:::note
Goal: First solo activities done, business-building habits started, 30-day review complete.

:::

- [ ]  Complete Module 5 — Building your business (read objection handling twice)
- [ ]  Run your first solo buyer showing — log it in FUB same day
- [ ]  Run or assist an open house — follow up every contact within 24 hours
- [ ]  FUB database at 100+ contacts with tasks set on every active lead
- [ ]  30-day review with your Mentor — bring this checklist, fully completed

---

:::note
Reality check — You will feel overwhelmed. Every agent on this team felt exactly what you're feeling. The agents who succeed here aren't necessarily the most talented — they're the most consistent. Trust the system, follow the checklist, ask for help early.

:::
