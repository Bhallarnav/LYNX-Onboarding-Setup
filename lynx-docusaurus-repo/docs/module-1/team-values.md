## Who we are

Lynx Real Estate Group is a Calgary-based residential team serving buyers and sellers across the city and surrounding areas — **Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere, High River, Bearspaw, and Springbank**.

We’re a team of agents who believe that *how you treat people matters as much as the result you get*.

We aren’t the biggest team in Calgary. We’re building toward being the **most trusted** one. Every client who works with a Lynx agent should feel like they have someone genuinely in their corner — not someone moving them through a process.

## What we stand for

:::note
**Commitment**

:::

:::note

## The non-negotiables

- **Speed to lead — always**
    
    Every new lead gets a call attempt within 5 minutes during business hours. If you can’t call, text immediately.
    
- **CRM discipline — if it’s not in FUB, it didn’t happen**
    
    Every call, text, showing, and conversation gets logged. Your pipeline is only as useful as your notes.
    
- **Client communication — proactive, not reactive**
    
    Clients should never have to chase you for an update. A short “nothing to report yet, but here’s where we’re at” message takes 30 seconds and builds enormous trust.
    
- **Professionalism — in person and online**
    
    How you show up — to showings, appointments, and on social media — reflects on every agent at Lynx. When in doubt, ask before posting. Dress like you’re already at the level you want to reach.
