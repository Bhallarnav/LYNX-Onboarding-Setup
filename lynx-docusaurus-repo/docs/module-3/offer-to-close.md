## The moment an offer is accepted

:::info
🎯 The first 30 minutes matter most.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>What to do immediately</strong></summary>

- Call your buyer (never text)  
- Confirm deposit (due within 24 hours)  
- Notify mortgage broker  
- Book home inspection (same day)  
- Log everything in FUB  
- Set all critical dates as tasks  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Send your buyer the timeline</strong></summary>

“Here’s what the next few weeks look like:

- Deposit due: [date]  
- Inspection: [date]  
- Financing deadline: [date]  
- Condition removal: [date]  
- Possession: [date]  

I’ll guide you through each step.”

👉 Removes uncertainty immediately.

</details>

---

## The deposit

:::info
🎯 Typically due within 24 hours in Alberta.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>How to explain it</strong></summary>

“It goes into the listing brokerage’s trust account — not to the seller.”

Confirm:
- method  
- timing  
- receipt  

Log it in FUB.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>If deposit is at risk</strong></summary>

Call listing agent immediately.

👉 This is a Mentor situation if unsure.

</details>

---

## The home inspection

:::info
🎯 Book it SAME DAY.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>Your role</strong></summary>

- Attend  
- Stay calm  
- Take notes/photos  
- Ask inspector to clarify severity  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How to interpret</strong></summary>

3 categories:

- Safety issues → serious  
- Maintenance → normal  
- Cosmetic → ignore  

👉 Help buyer separate emotion from reality.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Next steps</strong></summary>

- Proceed  
- Negotiate  
- Walk away  

👉 Buyer decides — you guide.

</details>

---

## The financing condition

:::info
🎯 Stay proactive — don’t wait.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>During financing</strong></summary>

- Check with broker every 2 days  
- Ensure buyer responds quickly  
- Flag issues immediately  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>If financing fails</strong></summary>

Call buyer immediately.

Options:
- Exit  
- Extension  
- Alternative financing  

👉 Call Mitch.

</details>

---

## Condition removal

:::info
🎯 This is when the deal becomes firm.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>How to explain</strong></summary>

“Once you sign — deposit is at risk if you walk away.”

Wait for clear confirmation.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>If deadline is missed</strong></summary>

Deal can collapse.

👉 Call Mitch immediately.

</details>

---

## Between removal and possession

:::info
🎯 Quiet period ≠ no communication.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>What must happen</strong></summary>

- Lawyer engaged  
- Insurance arranged  
- Final walkthrough scheduled  
- Utilities arranged  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Weekly check-in</strong></summary>

“Everything is on track — let me know if you need anything.”

👉 Prevents anxiety.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Final walkthrough</strong></summary>

Confirm:
- Inclusions present  
- Condition unchanged  
- No damage  

If issues → call listing agent immediately.

</details>

---

## Possession day

:::info
🎯 Keys usually released after funds transfer.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>Your role</strong></summary>

- Stay available  
- Communicate updates  
- Call when keys released  
- Meet buyer if possible  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Your role is NOT</strong></summary>

- Moving coordinator  
- Legal fixer  
- Locksmith  

👉 Escalate when needed.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>If something goes wrong</strong></summary>

Call listing agent → then Mitch.

Stay calm. Communicate clearly.

</details>

---

## After possession

:::info
🎯 This is where referrals are built.
:::

<details>
<summary><strong>Follow-up system</strong></summary>

Day 7 → check-in + review ask  
Day 30 → follow-up  
Month 6 → market update  
Month 12 → anniversary call  

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Review ask</strong></summary>

“Would mean a lot if you left a quick review — here’s the link.”

👉 Send via text.

</details>

---

## What can go wrong

:::note
💡 Common issues:

- Financing fails  
- Inspection issues  
- Seller delays possession  
- Buyer wants to walk after firm  

👉 Every one = call Mitch immediately.
:::

- [ ] Buyer fully understands  
- [ ] Signed + sent

- [ ] Lawyer + insurance

- [ ] Available  
- [ ] Keys confirmed

💡 Common mistakes:

- Not booking inspection same day  
- Going quiet during conditions  
- Missing deadlines  
- Not sending timeline  
- Trying to solve issues alone  
- Disappearing after possession
